About Us
Kumari Plant Nursery is located in an ideal location in the southern part of Sri Lanka. Where it receives an average rainfall of 2147 mm and a average temperature is 27oC and it is most suitable for growing tropical plants. Kumari Nursery is a wholesaler and retailer of Orchids, Anthuriums, Aglonemas and Other Plants.

The nursery was started in 2011 and is gradually growing and currently cultivating 1000 sq.Ft. Orchid , 675 sq.Ft. Anthurium and 510 sq.Ft. Aglaonema are grown in Nethouse. These plants are planted following eco-friendly farming practices. Anthurium,orchids are also grown as cut flowers and plants. Floriculture started small in 2011 and has been going on for 12 years now.
